About


A website for food logging, designed to help me keep track of what I eat, understand the ingredients I'm consuming, and get meal ideas + inspiration. The site will explore means of health and self-care while balancing college life.


While balancing work and school I’ve found myself often skipping out on meals or eating processed/junk foods that are quick to make. I also find myself overbuying or underbuying ingredients and having trouble budgeting and choosing what to eat. I wanted to make a “diary” that not only pushes me to eat cleaner but to also help me organize my relationship with food and finances.

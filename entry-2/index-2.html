A website for food logging, designed to help me keep track of what I eat, understand the ingredients I am consuming, and get meal ideas and inspiration. The site will explore means of health and self care while balancing college life.
While balancing work and school I have found myself often skipping out on meals or eating processed or junk foods that are quick to make. I also find myself overbuying or underbuying ingredients and having trouble budgeting and choosing what to eat. I wanted to make a “diary” that not only pushes me to eat cleaner but to also help me organize my relationship with food and finances.
